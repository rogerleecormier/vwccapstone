CODING Hobby to Grow Professionally

Development has been a long-term hobby of mine, one that has spanned a good 20 years. While I have never become a master of any language, I have become proficient enough to be dangerous when diving into code, often modifying already existing code pieces to fit the needs and expectations of the project I am working on. I have taken courses in VB.NET, Java, and HTML/CSS, and I am self-taught in Python.

My love for web development has been my longest hobby, one that has seen me setting up sites with Joomla, Wordpress, Jekyll, Bootstrap, Tailwind, and now MKDocs with Jinja2. I have toyed with other frameworks and libraries, such as Next.JS, Gatsby, Drupal, and JQuery, but I have never became a master of any. I have a strong interest in Static Site Generators, and I hope to immerse myself into more experiences with these types of frameworks.
